HOW CHOCOLATES ARE MADE !!

Chocolates they are so sweet , creamy and crunchy. Ever wondered how they are made ?? This Paragraph will tell you about how chocolates are made

The sweet chocolates come from the cocoa tree . The cocoa tree has beans on it which are called COCOA . The cocoa beans have seeds inside it . The seeds are then separeted and fermented and dried for 5 days . The seeds are now dried . They are grinded into soft and smoothy liquid chocolate . Now the chocolate is poured into the containers and freezed in the freezer . After they freezed we are ready to eat !!
